About Me

Personal

I am a third year Computer Engineering major at the University of Cincinnati, currently pursuing my bachelors degree, and am interested in software development - open to all platforms.

Goals

  • After I complete my bachelor's degree, I may attend graduate school for my masters.
  • Professionally, I want to perform valued work which creates a positive impact on others.

Involvement

  • UC Alliance: I've been a member of UC Alliance since I started college, where I've interacted with a diverse group of individuals of different backgrounds, expanding my cultural awareness.
  • ACM@UC: I joined ACM@UC my Sophomore year, where I've met other students with an interest in technology. As well, it's given me a look into other specialities, and areas of interest for my future studies.
  • ACM-W: I joined ACM-W my third year through common connections with the goal of helping to increase the number of under represented populations in technology.
  • RevolutionUC: I joined the RevolutionUC organization team when I joined ACM@UC. RevolutionUC is the University of Cincinnati's 24 hour, student ran hackathon. I was part of the logistics team, where we were responsible for much of the organization and planning.

Beliefs

I am a fan of open source and believe that by contributing to it, we can assist others in learning new skills. I am also an advocate for inclusive communities and a believer of furthering diversification. By welcoming various groups and expanding our communities, we increase our own knowledge by allowing new ideas from varying backgrounds.
